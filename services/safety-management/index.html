Safety Management & Certification

At MM Custom we put ourselves in your shoes, we adapt our services to meet your company’s unique needs. We can help you set up the safety program that fits your industry and way of working. The Alberta Certificate of Recognition (COR) is awarded to employers who develop efficient health and safety management systems which meet the provincial standards. These certificates are issued by Alberta Labour and are co-signed by the Certifying Partner in partnership with the Alberta government.

Advantages of COR

A COR Certification is recognition that a company’s health and safety management system has been evaluated by a certified COR auditor and found to meet all provincial safety standards. There are many benefits to obtaining a COR certification including

  • Reducing workplace injuries and improving workplace morale.
  • Earning a financial incentive through the WCB Partnerships in Injury Reduction Program (PIR). You can earn up to 20% off your industry WCB rate by obtaining a COR Certification.
  • In Alberta, many companies now require that you have a valid COR in order to bid on jobs, especially within the oil and gas and construction industries.

Setting up COR for your company

Setting your company up with a COR is a comprehensive process which begins with setting up a health and safety management program. We can make this process simple and easy for you. Here are the steps we help you take to get started.

  • Call for your FREE one hour consultation.
  • Together we will determine where your safety program is at and add in what you need or provide you with your entire program.
  • We complete the setup from beginning to end of your COR certification.
  • Not only do we set you up with your program, we go through all the steps of finding the right COR Partnership for your company and keep providing information and support until you have your COR.
  • We not only provide you with a program, we become your personal Safety Professional and you and your company are welcome to contact us ongoing with any safety questions and we will provide you with all the support you need!
  • Lastly, you will need a COR external audit. We can help you find a certified external auditor to audit your program.
  • We perform yearly internal COR audits.

Program details

You will receive (both electronically and in a safety binder)

  • One HSE safety manual.
  • Safety forms
  • Safe work practices and procedures.
  • Formal hazard assessments.
  • Inventory of equipment.
  • Job descriptions.
  • Company organizational chart.
  • Training & form matrix.
